Tesla Stock Prediction

Forecasting a notoriously noisy signal.

A time-series model forecasting TSLA closing prices from historical OHLCV data. Heavy on feature engineering across technical indicators, driven by exploratory data analysis to pick the right model — and honest about where prediction meets uncertainty.
